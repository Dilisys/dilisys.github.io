03. Ninpek| Unidentified Fun Object

It's ninja time! Run and jump to reach the end of the world!
— In-game description

Somewhere between Touhou and the airship levels of Mario 3, here's Ninpek! A weird amalgamation of ideas from all across retro gaming, some of which may not be all that obvious from the first couple of playthroughs. Ninpek continues the themes of eggs, adventure, and the strange interstitials between life and death, where your purgatorial state is not necessarily something that's a disadvantage.
